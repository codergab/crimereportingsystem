Matric No: 16010211037 Project Name: Crime Report System Project Supervisor: Mr E.A Biya Project Description:

A crime reporting system based on Android operating system, to help persuading citizens in order to reduce hate and distress on law enforcement agencies due to the violently act which is believed the bodies are acted upon. So therefore the public can give detailed information about crime scenes and neighbourhood watch observations happening around their vicinity without visiting a particular location.
